Don't be surprised if JWDWeb sporadically disappears from the internet for a while (a few days?) as I attempt to upgrade its Gentoo Linux server.  While Gentoo allows me to keep an up to date, optimized for speed server, it also can be a pain to do a massive update.  I would feel less pain if I upgraded more often, but I just don't do it unless there is an applicable security hole or I want the latest of a large software package.  The reason for upgrading is the latter this time.  I'm learning about j2ee and am installing open source implementations of j2ee components - Tomcat, JBoss, etc.
