The BSG series is moving along. I'm really enjoying it. If you haven't already, give a look at the SciFi.com web site: http://www.scifi.com/battlestar.

Specifically, you may like the BSG blog. If you are really into the show, you'll love the podcast. If you don't know what podcasting is, first try to keep up with the times, second read about it here. The podcast is an audio commentary to the latest episode by executive producer Ronald D. More.

The site also has episode guides, deleted scenes, etc.
