I like the idea of Google's Project Glass, but the jury is still out on how practical that will be.  But a watch.. that is something different.  While I gave up my watch when I got my always-with-me-iPhone, I've missed the at a glance information a watch can give.  These new line of "smart watches" being kick started look very promising.
 
I suspect one of these smart watches will be on my wrist by the end of the year:
http://getpebble.com/
http://www.metawatch.org/

I like the e-ink of the Pebble, but I like the features/compatibility of the Metawatch Strata.  If I had to pick based on the available information, I'd go with the Strata .  What do you think?
