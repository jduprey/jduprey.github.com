Winter weather causes numerous accidents

Posted at: 01/13/2012 8:07 AM

| Updated at: 01/13/2012 8:33 AM

News10NBC is hearing reports of dozens of accidents around Monroe County throughout Friday morning. A string of minor crashes were reported beginning around 7 a.m. and lasting throughout the morning commute hours.

Right now, counties in western and central New York are under a Winter Weather Advisory until 7 p.m. Friday night.
