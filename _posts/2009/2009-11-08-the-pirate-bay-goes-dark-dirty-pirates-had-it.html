At the end of August the Stockholm district court ordered Black Internet to shut down the connection that The Pirate Bay used, and the ISP complied.

via computerworld.com

The Pirate Bay kinda reminded me of Larry Flint (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Larry_Flynt) in that while they kinda represented the underbelly of what is considered socially acceptable, they stood up to "the man" .. and for that they got my respect. That and I occasionally needed their services.. for perfectly legitimate uses of course. I suspect this is not the last that we see of them. I also suspect that the movie industry will get a clue as the music industry seems to have done.
