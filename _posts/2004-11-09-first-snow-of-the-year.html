We had our first snow of the year and it actually "stuck" ... kinda. While I still find myself wondering what happened to summer, autumn has all but passed and we are staring down winter. We had our first snow yesterday. It came down light and fluffy, think snow globe, and some of it actually stuck to the ground for a while. The last couple of weekends I've enjoyed the smell of dry, brightly colored leaves. Winter, it seems, is on its way.
