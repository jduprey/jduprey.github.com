Its 11:58 PM, Wednesday. Abby is scheduled for a C-section 9:30 AM Friday, March 11. All is quiet, Abby is well, the baby is well if not exactly cooperating with the birthing process. However, I can feel this anxiety building within me - anticipation of this life changing event. Well let it happen and be done with it. This waiting, and anticipating, and worrying is killing me! It was time for us to have a child. I have no idea exactly how it will change things...us... me, I just know that it will. So be it. Fates that be, just let her be healthy, please!

There. I feel better. :)
