jduprey

February 20th, 2006

iDuprey.com enjoyed another hiatus from the internet on Saturday due to a power outage.  A very large tree decided to rest on our power lines in the back yard.  It took them all day to remove the tree and turn the power back on.  We fired up the generator to keep us warm, but the site did not rank so high to deserve electricity too.  Power was lost around 11 AM on Saturday and returned around 11 PM.  Lets hope we have no more interruptions for a while.  The power crew ripped the cable line off our house and left it lying on the ground.  Fortunately, this did not impact our cable or internet service.  We will be filing a complaint with RG&E later this week.

I'll post some interesting pictures of the tree later.

-John
