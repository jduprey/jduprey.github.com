January 28th, 2006
Emma took her first, documented, steps yesterday evening.  I say "documented" because she has been standing and lunging for things this past week, but this is the first time we got her on video actually stepping.  Earlier in the day she had made steps, but I didn't have the camera out!  Emma is 10 months old.  This is a major milestone!
