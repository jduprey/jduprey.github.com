For those that track calories and LOVE SoCo here is the official word on carbs and cals for their 70 and 100 proof liquor...

Thank you for visiting our website and for your interest in Southern Comfort.

  

 

A 1-ounce serving of 70 proof Southern Comfort contains 2.7 grams of carbohydrates and 65 calories. A 1-ounce serving of 100 proof Southern Comfort contains 3.4 grams of carbohydrates and 95 calories. Southern Comfort contains no fats, sulfites, or gluten. I hope this information helps.
