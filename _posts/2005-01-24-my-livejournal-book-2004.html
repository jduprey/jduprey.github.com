LJBook is a service that can convert your LiveJournal entries into a PDF document. Its quite nice. I plan to use this service every year. You can get my journal entries in book form here: LiveJournal2004Public.pdf. Why you would want it, is something you will have to answer for yourself. I want it for posterity sake. These journal entries are a time capsule of me, and my view of the world at a particular point in time.. *sigh*

The only thing I don't like about it is that I have to supply my LiveJournal username and password, unencrypted even. This should be a premium service that LiveJournal provides, in my opinion.

Anyway, you can try LJBook for yourself:
